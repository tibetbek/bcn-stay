Our Story

A Reflection of Barcelona

Nestled in the winding streets of the Gothic Quarter, Hotel Mirall occupies a meticulously restored 19th-century palace. Each of our 32 rooms is a curated blend of Catalan heritage and contemporary design — where exposed stone arches meet sleek modern furnishings.

From our celebrated rooftop terrace with panoramic views of the Cathedral to our intimate spa tucked within the building's original cellars, every corner of Hotel Mirall tells a story. We don't just host guests — we create memories.
